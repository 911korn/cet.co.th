About CET (Thailand)

CET (Thailand) Company Limited is a Thai-registered technology company headquartered in Bangkok. We design and develop reliable software, mobile applications, and AI-powered products from concept to launch.

Our team focuses on clean engineering, thoughtful product design, and long-term reliability. We publish our own products and also partner with select clients on custom builds.
